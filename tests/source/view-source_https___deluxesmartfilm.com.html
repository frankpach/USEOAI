INSTANT TOTAL PRIVACY

Smart Film is a high-tech seamless invention that changes from a transparent state to an opaque one with a movement of a switch.

AESTHETIC IMPROVEMENT

Intelligent technology provides unprecedented control over the amount of light, privacy and heat that enters a space and is ideal for medical and automotive facade applications.

HIGH LIGHT TRANSMISSION

They use advanced technology to adjust transparency according to the user's needs, offering an unlimited number of applications and utilities. When it is turned off, it offers total privacy thanks to its opacity. And when you turn it on, it becomes transparent. In both modes, he continues to maintain a excellent light transmission from one room to another.
